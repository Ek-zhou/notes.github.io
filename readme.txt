== Description ==
Exort is ultimate, super flexible and fully responsive WordPress theme,
suited for business websites, entertainment websites, shop websites,
and users who want to showcase their work on a neat portfolio site.
You can simply import the full demo contents then replace the content with your own.
It allows you to build your site in seconds and spend more time for your business.
